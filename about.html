About this project

How to Train Your Chatbot is a playful explainer about large language models (LLMs) and conversational agents (also known as AI chatbots), aimed at demystifying the technology to build critical tech literacy amongst its wide range of users.

This site was built by Decifer Studio, a project of Alia ElKattan and Lujain Ibrahim, with the support of the incredible team below:
